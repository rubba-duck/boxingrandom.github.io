Cookie Clicker

Fullscreen

Indulge in the addictive sweetness of Cookie Clicker, a delightful browser-based game that will have you clicking for cookies in no time. With its simple yet captivating gameplay, Cookie Clicker offers endless cookie-producing fun directly in your web browser.

How to Play

Embark on your cookie-clicking adventure using these simple steps:

  • Start by clicking on the giant cookie to produce cookies – each click yields one additional cookie.
  • Accumulate cookies to purchase upgrades and expand your workforce, gradually building your cookie empire.
  • Initially, rely on manual clicking, but as your cookie empire grows, automated upgrades will handle the work for you, ensuring a steady stream of cookies.

Delve into two categories of upgrades – Buildings and Upgrades:

  • Buildings automate cookie production, allowing you to increase your cookie count without manual intervention.
  • Upgrades enhance clicking efficiency and cookie generation, accelerating your progress and leading to the creation of a cookie empire of epic proportions.
