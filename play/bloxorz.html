Bloxorz

Fullscreen

Bloxorz is a captivating puzzle game that challenges players with intricate levels and mind-bending obstacles. Designed to be played directly within a web browser, Bloxorz offers a convenient and accessible gaming experience for puzzle enthusiasts of all skill levels.

Controls

To navigate through the complex maze-like levels of Bloxorz, players only need to utilize their arrow keys. With simple yet precise control over the movement of the block, you can maneuver it through each level, avoiding pitfalls and obstacles along the way. Mastering the use of your arrow keys is essential for success in this challenging puzzle game.
