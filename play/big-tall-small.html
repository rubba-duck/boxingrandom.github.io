Welcome to "Big Tall Small," an engaging browser-based game that challenges your spatial awareness and problem-solving skills! Immerse yourself in a world where size matters, and maneuver your way through various obstacles to reach your goals. With intuitive controls and captivating gameplay, "Big Tall Small" offers a delightful gaming experience for players of all ages.

Controls

Move: WAD or arrow keys
Switch: down arrow key/space bar/enter
Reset: R
Back: Esc or B
