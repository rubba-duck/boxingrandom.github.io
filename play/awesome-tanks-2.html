Awesome Tanks 2

Fullscreen

Prepare for explosive tank warfare in "Awesome Tanks 2," the action-packed sequel to the popular tank battle game. With its intense battles, customizable tanks, and challenging levels, "Awesome Tanks 2" puts players in command of a powerful tank as they navigate treacherous terrain and engage in fierce combat against enemy forces. Get ready to unleash devastation on your foes and prove your tank-driving prowess in this adrenaline-fueled adventure.

Game Guide

Here's a guide to the controls in "Awesome Tanks 2":

Controls

WASD or arrow keys to move
Left mouse button to shoot
Number keys to switch weapon

With intuitive controls at your fingertips, you'll be able to maneuver your tank with precision and unleash a barrage of firepower upon your enemies. Utilize different weapons and strategic movements to dominate the battlefield and emerge victorious in "Awesome Tanks 2." Get ready to roll out and crush your opposition!
