Doodle Jump

Fullscreen

Embark on a thrilling adventure of vertical ascent with Doodle Jump, a beloved browser-based game that offers endless jumping fun. With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Doodle Jump provides hours of entertainment directly in your web browser.

How to Play

Engage in the endless excitement of Doodle Jump with these simple instructions:

  • Move your Doodle Jumper left to right to navigate through platforms, aiming to ascend as high as possible.
  • Left-click to shoot and unleash your Doodle Jumper's full potential, helping you overcome obstacles and reach new heights.
  • Challenge yourself to achieve your highest score by continuously jumping for as long as possible, dodging obstacles and collecting power-ups along the way.
