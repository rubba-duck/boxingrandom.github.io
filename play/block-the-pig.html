Block The Pig

Fullscreen

Get ready for a challenging puzzle experience with Block the Pig, a captivating browser-based game that puts your strategic thinking to the test. With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Block the Pig offers hours of entertainment for players of all ages.

Instructions

In Block the Pig, your objective is to prevent the pig from escaping by strategically building walls around it. Click on a tile to place a block and keep placing blocks until you've surrounded the pig completely. Be mindful not to let the pig reach one of the edge tiles, or your efforts will be in vain!

Strategic Gameplay

Each move in Block the Pig requires careful planning and foresight. Analyze the pig's movements and anticipate its next steps to effectively block its path. With each block placed, you inch closer to victory—but one wrong move could spell disaster. Challenge yourself to find the optimal strategy and outsmart the pig at every turn.

Test Your Wits

Block the Pig offers a variety of challenging levels that will put your puzzle-solving skills to the test. As you progress, you'll encounter increasingly complex scenarios that require creativity and precision to overcome. Can you master the art of blocking the pig and emerge victorious in every level?
