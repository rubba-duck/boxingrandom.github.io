Drift Hunters

Fullscreen

Embark on an adrenaline-fueled journey into the world of street racing with "Drift Hunters." This browser-based game offers an immersive experience where players can customize their rides, master the art of drifting, and compete in intense races.

Controls

Master your vehicle with intuitive controls:

  • Steer: WASD or arrow keys
  • Handbrake: Spacebar
  • Camera: C
  • Shift up gears: Left shift
  • Shift down gears: Left Ctrl
