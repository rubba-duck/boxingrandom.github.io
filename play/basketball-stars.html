Basketball Stars

Fullscreen

Welcome to "Basketball Stars," an exhilarating online game that brings the fast-paced action of basketball to your web browser! Compete against friends or challenge the computer in intense one-on-one basketball matches. With simple controls and addictive gameplay, Basketball Stars guarantees hours of fun and excitement.

Player One Controls:

WASD - Move
B - Shoot/Perform action
S - Pump
V - Super-shot
D twice - Dash

Player Two Controls:

Arrow keys - Move
L - Shoot/Perform action
DOWN Arrow - Pump
K - Super-shot
LEFT Arrow twice - Dash
