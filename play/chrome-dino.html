Chrome Dino

Fullscreen

Experience the thrill of endless running with Chrome Dino, a classic browser-based game that challenges players to navigate through obstacles and achieve high scores. With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Chrome Dino provides entertainment directly in your web browser.

Controls

Master the art of survival using these straightforward controls:

  • Press the space bar or the up arrow key to make the dinosaur jump, avoiding obstacles and leaping over obstacles in its path.
  • Utilize the down arrow key to make the dinosaur duck, allowing it to pass through low-hanging obstacles with ease.
