Burnin' Rubber 5 Xs

Fullscreen

Get ready for high-octane action with Burnin' Rubber 5 XS, an adrenaline-pumping browser-based game that will satisfy your need for speed and destruction. With its fast-paced gameplay and explosive features, Burnin' Rubber 5 XS delivers thrilling racing and combat directly to your web browser.

Controls

Take control of your vehicle and dominate the road using these intuitive controls:

  • Drive your car with precision using the WASD keys or arrow keys, maneuvering through tight corners and dodging enemy attacks.
  • Unleash devastation upon your opponents by firing weapons with the Z and X keys or the left and right mouse buttons, strategically eliminating rivals and clearing your path to victory.
  • Master the art of drifting by holding down the Shift key, sliding gracefully around corners and gaining an edge over your competitors.
