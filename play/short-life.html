Short Life

Fullscreen

Embark on a perilous journey through a series of deadly obstacles and traps in Short Life, an intense and thrilling game designed for the web browser. With its challenging gameplay and unique mechanics, Short Life offers an adrenaline-pumping experience for players seeking excitement and adventure. Navigate through hazardous environments and test your skills to survive in this action-packed game.

Controls

Move left and right: Left and right arrow keys

Jump / stand up: Up arrow (if crouching)

Crouch: Down arrow
