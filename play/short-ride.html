Short Ride

Fullscreen

Prepare for a wild and adventurous ride in Short Ride, an action-packed game that will put your skills to the test. Suitable for playing directly in your web browser, Short Ride offers a thrilling experience that will keep you on the edge of your seat. With its fast-paced gameplay and challenging obstacles, this game promises excitement and adrenaline-fueled thrills for players of all ages.

How to Play

Move left and right: Left and right arrow keys / A, D

Accelerate: Up arrow / W

Get on / off bicycle: Space bar
