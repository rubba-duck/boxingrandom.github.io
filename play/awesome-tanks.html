Awesome Tanks

Fullscreen

Prepare for an epic tank battle in Awesome Tanks, an action-packed browser-based game that puts you in command of a powerful tank on a mission to defeat enemy forces and destroy their base. With intuitive controls and strategic gameplay, Awesome Tanks offers an exhilarating gaming experience suitable for players of all skill levels.

Instructions

In Awesome Tanks, maneuver your tank using the arrow keys or WASD keys and aim your weapon with your mouse. Engage enemy tanks, collect coins, and obliterate the enemy base to progress through each level. Utilize your earnings wisely to upgrade your tank's armor, weapons, visibility, and more, enhancing your chances of victory in the battlefield.

Master Tank Warfare

Success in Awesome Tanks hinges on your ability to move and fire simultaneously. Practice controlling your tank efficiently to outmaneuver enemy forces and emerge victorious. With outnumbered odds and relentless enemies, quick reflexes and strategic thinking are essential to survival.

Strategic Tips

Maximize your chances of success in Awesome Tanks by prioritizing certain upgrades. Increase your visibility early on to target and eliminate tank spawners from a distance, reducing the number of enemies you'll face.
