Temple Run 2

Fullscreen
Temple Run 2, developed by Imangi, invites players into an exhilarating infinite running adventure. Escape from your relentless enemy while navigating through treacherous obstacles and traps strewn along the path. Traverse cliffs, forests, and treacherous mines as you aid a fearless treasure hunter in his quest to escape with the coveted gold idol. But beware, the sinister monkey monsters are in hot pursuit! Are you ready to embark on this pulse-pounding adrenaline-fueled journey?

Play Temple Run 2 Online

Experience the thrill of Temple Run 2 directly in your web browser, no downloads required. Ideal for Chromebook computers, enjoy fullscreen mode on the large screen of your computer, offering a more immersive experience compared to playing on a phone. Say goodbye to interruptions from ads before and during the game, and dive straight into the action!

How to Play Temple Run 2

Keyboard Controls:

  • Move - AD or Left/Right arrow keys
  • Jump - W or Up arrow key
  • Slide down - S or Down arrow key

Mobile Controls:

  • Move and turn - Swipe left / right
  • Jump - Swipe up
  • Slide - Swipe down

Creator and Installments

Temple Run 2, the sequel to the mega-hit Temple Run, was created by Imangi. Immerse yourself in their legendary endless runner games such as Temple Run 2: Holi Festival, Temple Run 2: Frozen Shadows, and Temple Run 2: Jungle Fall!

Completing Temple Run 2

To conquer Temple Run 2, collect free coins, upgrade your character stats, time your jumps meticulously over double-jump obstacles, and strive to fulfill stage objectives.

Temple Run 2 on PC

Enjoy Temple Run 2 on your PC directly through your browser, completely free of charge and without any downloads or installations required.

Gameplay and Features

As an endless running game, Temple Run 2 doesn't have a definitive end. Players continue until their character collides into a large obstacle, falls into the water, or is overtaken by the demon monkeys relentlessly chasing them.

Monsters and Power-ups

The main antagonists of Temple Run 2 are the Evil Demon Monkeys, pursuing the player in their relentless quest to retrieve the golden idol. Collect coins to fill the power meter, unlocking temporary speed and invincibility boosts to aid in your escape.

Powering Up in Temple Run 2

To enhance your abilities in Temple Run 2, access "Abilities" and tap on the power you wish to improve, whether it's Shield Duration, Coin Magnet, or Boost Distance.
