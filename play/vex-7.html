Vex 7

Fullscreen

Prepare for an exhilarating platforming experience with Vex 7, a challenging game that will test your agility and precision. Perfect for playing directly in your web browser, Vex 7 offers an addictive gameplay experience that will keep you coming back for more. With its intricate levels and clever obstacles, this game provides a thrilling challenge for players of all skill levels.

How to Play

Controls: Use the Arrow Keys or WASD to move. To enter a level, press DOWN or S on top of the level block.

Master the Levels:

Your goal is to run and jump through the level while avoiding the obstacles. A lot of the level is going to be wall jumps, sliding, and maybe some swimming. Be precise and move at the right time to avoid the obstacles and get to the end. Stay on your toes, there will be hazards around every corner.

Challenge Yourself:

If you're up for a challenge, each level has optional criteria to earn more stars. While it isn't necessary to collect every single star, it makes the game a much more fun yet difficult challenge. Earn all three stars in a level to unlock the hardcore version.
