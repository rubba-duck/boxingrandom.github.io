Boxing Random

Fullscreen

Step into the ring and unleash your boxing prowess in Boxing Random, an electrifying browser-based game that delivers intense boxing matches. Whether you're facing off against the computer or challenging a friend, Boxing Random offers fast-paced action and excitement directly in your web browser.

Single Player Controls

Master your boxing skills with these controls:

  • Use the up arrow key to maneuver your boxer around the ring, dodging punches and landing powerful blows on your opponent.

Two Players Controls

Challenge a friend to a thrilling boxing match with these control options:

  • Player 1: Utilize the up arrow key to control your boxer's movements, aiming to outmaneuver and outstrike your opponent.
  • Player 2: Coordinate your attacks using both the up arrow key and the W key, strategically positioning your boxer for maximum impact.
