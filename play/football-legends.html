Football Legends

Fullscreen

Experience the thrill of soccer greatness with "Football Legends," an exciting browser game that lets you become a football star. With its fast-paced gameplay and competitive matches, Football Legends offers endless excitement for soccer enthusiasts.

Controls

Move: Use WASD or Arrow keys to navigate your player on the field.

Shoot: Press L or X to take a shot at the goal.

Supershot: Activate your Supershot ability by pressing K or Z for an extra powerful kick.
