A Small World Cup

Fullscreen

Step into the world of miniature soccer with "A Small World Cup," where tiny players compete on a pint-sized pitch for glory and victory. This charming game brings all the excitement and intensity of a real-world soccer tournament to a scaled-down, whimsical setting. With its intuitive controls and captivating gameplay, "A Small World Cup" offers endless entertainment for players of all ages.

Game Guide

Here's a guide to the controls in "A Small World Cup":

Controls

Drag and release to shoot.

With just a simple drag and release mechanism, you can unleash powerful shots and guide your team to championship glory. So gather your miniature squad, hone your skills, and prepare to experience the excitement of soccer on a small scale in "A Small World Cup!"
