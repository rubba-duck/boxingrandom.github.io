Blumgi Rocket

Fullscreen

Embark on an exhilarating journey through space with Blumgi Rocket, a thrilling browser-based game that promises excitement and challenges for players of all ages. With its simple yet captivating gameplay, Blumgi Rocket offers an accessible and enjoyable gaming experience directly in your web browser.

How to Play Blumgi Rocket

Main Controls

To maneuver your rocket through the vast expanse of space, use the following controls:

  • Drive - W/S or Up/Down arrow keys
  • Rocket - Space bar

Alternative Controls

If you prefer alternative control schemes, you can also use:

  • Drive - A
  • Rocket - D
