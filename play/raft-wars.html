Raft Wars

Fullscreen

Dive into a thrilling adventure with Raft Wars, an exciting browser-based game that pits you against waves of enemies as you defend your treasure alongside Simon and his brother. Armed with a tennis ball launcher, your objective is to protect your raft by knocking enemies off their perch. With simple controls and addictive gameplay, Raft Wars offers hours of entertainment suitable for playing on a web browser.

Instructions

In Raft Wars, use your mouse to aim and fire your tennis ball launcher at incoming enemies. Strategize your shots to knock enemies off their perch and prevent them from reaching your raft. Stay vigilant, as getting knocked out of your raft will result in game over. Can you defend your treasure against the relentless onslaught of enemies?

Defend Your Treasure

Join Simon and his brother in an epic battle to protect their treasure from invaders. As you progress through the game, face increasingly challenging waves of enemies determined to seize your riches. Utilize your skills and cunning to outwit and outshoot your adversaries, emerging victorious in the ultimate treasure defense.
