Basketball Legends

Fullscreen

Step onto the virtual court and showcase your basketball skills in "Basketball Legend," a thrilling game suitable for playing on any web browser. Whether you're a solo player looking to dominate the courts or you prefer head-to-head competition against a friend, "Basketball Legend" offers an exciting and immersive basketball experience that will keep you hooked for hours.

How to Play

1 Player Controls

  • AD or left and right arrow keys to move
  • X/L to shoot/steal
  • S to pump/block
  • A+A or D+D or left/right arrow twice to dash
  • K/Z to super shot

2 Player Controls

  • To move, player 1 use AD and player 2 use left and right arrow keys
  • To shoot/steal, player 1 use B and player 2 use L
  • To pump, player 1 use S and player 2 use down arrow
  • To dash, player 1 use A/D twice and player 2 use left/right arrow twice
  • K to super shot

Master these controls to dribble, shoot, steal, and dash your way to victory on the basketball court. Whether you're playing solo or against a friend, "Basketball Legend" promises an exhilarating and competitive basketball experience that will keep you coming back for more. Are you ready to become the ultimate basketball legend?
