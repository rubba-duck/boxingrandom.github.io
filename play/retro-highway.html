Retro Highway

Fullscreen

Retro Highway brings the thrill of retro-style arcade racing to your web browser, offering an exhilarating experience reminiscent of classic motorcycle racing games. With its charming pixel art graphics and fast-paced gameplay, Retro Highway promises endless hours of excitement for players of all ages. Get ready to rev your engines and hit the road in this addictive journey through nostalgic landscapes.

How to Play Retro Highway

Steer: Use W and D or the left and right arrow keys to navigate your motorcycle through the obstacles.

Nitro Boost: Hold down the spacebar to activate the nitro boost and propel yourself to greater speeds.
