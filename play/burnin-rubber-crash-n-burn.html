Burnin' Rubber Crash n' Burn

Fullscreen

Get ready to ignite the streets with chaos and destruction in Burnin' Rubber Crash n' Burn, an action-packed browser-based game that will leave you on the edge of your seat. With its intense gameplay and explosive features, Burnin' Rubber Crash n' Burn offers thrilling racing and combat directly in your web browser.

Controls

Take command of your vehicle and dominate the asphalt with these intuitive controls:

  • Steer your car with precision using the WASD keys or arrow keys, navigating through crowded streets and avoiding obstacles with skillful maneuvers.
  • Unleash havoc upon your adversaries by firing weapons with the Z key or left-click, obliterating opponents and clearing your path to victory.
  • Activate powerful special abilities with the X key or right-click, turning the tide of battle in your favor and leaving your opponents in the dust.
