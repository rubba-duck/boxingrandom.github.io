Temple Of Boom

Fullscreen

Embark on an explosive adventure in the Temple of Boom, a thrilling multiplayer game set in ancient ruins filled with danger and treasure. Suitable for playing directly in your web browser, Temple of Boom offers fast-paced action and strategic gameplay that will keep you on the edge of your seat. Join forces with friends or go solo as you navigate through traps, battle enemies, and collect valuable loot in this adrenaline-pumping quest.

Controls

Single Player:

  • Jump or Double Jump: Arrow Up
  • Move Sideways: Left/Right Arrow
  • Pick Up Weapon: Down Arrow
  • Shoot: Z
  • Switch Weapon: X

Two Players:

  • Player 1:
    • Jump or Double Jump: W
    • Move Sideways: A/S
    • Pick Up Weapon: S
    • Shoot: C
    • Switch Weapon: V
  • Player 2:
    • Jump or Double Jump: Arrow Up
    • Move Sideways: Left/Right Arrow
    • Pick Up Weapon: Down Arrow
    • Shoot: K
    • Switch Weapon: L
