Curve Ball 3D

Fullscreen

Dive into the immersive world of Curve Ball 3D, a captivating browser-based game that combines skill and precision to test your reflexes. With its dynamic gameplay and sleek graphics, Curve Ball 3D offers an exhilarating gaming experience directly in your web browser.

Controls

Master the art of curveball manipulation with these intuitive controls:

  • Launch the ball with a simple left click, sending it hurtling towards the playing field.
  • Maneuver your pedal by moving your mouse, guiding it to intercept the ball and keep the game alive.
