Tag

Fullscreen

Experience the classic game of Tag in a digital realm with the browser-based adaptation simply titled "Tag." Whether you're reminiscing about childhood memories or seeking some friendly competition, this game offers a fun and engaging experience for players of all ages. With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Tag provides a nostalgic twist on a timeless game.

How to Play Tag

Movement:

  • Player 1: W, A and D keys
  • Player 2: Up, Right and Left arrow keys
  • Player 3: I, J and L keys
  • Player 4: T, F and H keys
