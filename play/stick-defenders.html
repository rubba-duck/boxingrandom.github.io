Stick Defenders

Fullscreen

Immerse yourself in the world of defense strategy with Stick Defenders, a captivating game that challenges your tactical skills to protect your territory from invading enemies. Designed for seamless gameplay on web browsers, Stick Defenders offers a blend of strategy and action that will keep you engaged for hours on end. With its charming stick figure graphics and intuitive mechanics, this game provides a delightful experience for players of all ages.

How to Play Stick Defenders

Use your left mouse button or finger to select and drag units. Merge two identical units by dragging one onto the other. It's that simple!
