Get ready for another round of thrilling chase and evasion in Tag 2, an exciting sequel to the classic game of tag brought to life in your web browser. Whether you're looking for a quick dose of fun or a competitive challenge, Tag 2 delivers an immersive and entertaining experience for players of all ages. With its intuitive controls and fast-paced gameplay, this game promises endless hours of excitement and laughter.

How to Play Tag 2

Movement:

  • A and D: Use A and D to walk around!
  • W: Use the W-key to jump!
