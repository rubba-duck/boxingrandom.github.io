Getaway Shootout

Fullscreen

Prepare for adrenaline-fueled mayhem in "Getaway Shootout," a thrilling browser game that pits you against opponents in a frantic race to the finish. With its fast-paced action and intense competition, Getaway Shootout offers non-stop excitement for players seeking a thrilling challenge.

How to Play

Jump Left: Press W and I keys

Jump Right: Press E and O keys

Power-up: Press R and P keys to activate power-ups and gain an advantage over your opponents.
