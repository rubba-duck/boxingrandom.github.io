Death Chase

Fullscreen

Experience the thrill of high-speed racing and intense combat with Death Chase, an action-packed browser-based game that will keep you on the edge of your seat. With its adrenaline-fueled gameplay and dynamic challenges, Death Chase offers heart-pounding excitement directly in your web browser.

Controls

Take control of your vehicle and dominate the track using these intuitive controls:

  • Use the WASD keys or arrow keys to maneuver your vehicle, navigating through challenging terrain and outmaneuvering opponents.
  • Press Z to execute a daring jump or slam maneuver, allowing you to evade obstacles and thwart your adversaries.
  • Utilize the X key to launch rockets at your opponents, blasting them out of your path and clearing the way to victory.
  • Press R to restart the race, giving you another chance to conquer the track and emerge victorious.
