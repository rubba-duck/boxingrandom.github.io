Penalty Kick Online

Fullscreen

Experience the excitement of the soccer pitch with Penalty Kick Online, a thrilling browser-based game that puts your kicking and goalkeeping skills to the test. With intuitive controls and competitive gameplay, Penalty Kick Online offers endless fun for players of all skill levels.

Instructions

In Penalty Kick Online, players take turns as the kicker and the goalkeeper. As the kicker, simply click and swipe on the ball with your mouse to take a shot. Be cautious not to swipe too far, as you risk missing the goal. As the goalkeeper, move your cursor to block the shot and prevent the opposing team from scoring.

Gameplay Features

Challenge your friends or compete against players from around the world in exciting penalty kick matches. Earn extra shots by correctly solving math problems after scoring your first goal. With our new XP and leveling system, build your career and showcase your skills as you climb the ranks in online play. Complete achievements to fill your trophy case and establish yourself as a soccer legend!

Tips for Success

For aspiring goalkeepers, one key tip is to use the heels of your hands to block shots, rather than relying solely on your fingertips. Aim slightly above the ball to increase your chances of making a successful block. With practice and precision, you can become a formidable goalkeeper and lead your team to victory on the virtual pitch.
