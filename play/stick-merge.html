Stick Merge

Fullscreen

Indulge in the addictive world of Stick Merge, a captivating game where strategic thinking meets fast-paced action. Designed to be played seamlessly in your web browser, Stick Merge offers an immersive experience that challenges players to merge weapons and fend off relentless enemies. With its minimalist stick figure art style and engaging gameplay, this game provides endless entertainment for players of all skill levels.

How to Play Stick Merge?

  • Select and move weapons - Click and drag the left mouse button
  • Shoot enemies - Drag and move the mouse cursor
